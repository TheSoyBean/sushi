What is Sushi?

Sushi is a Japanese dish made with vinegared rice and various toppings. It can include seafood, vegetables, and eggs.

Types of Sushi

There are many different types of sushi, but here are a few of the most popular:

  • Nigiri-zushi: Slices of fish on top of pressed vinegared rice.
  • Maki-zushi: Sushi rolls made with seaweed (nori), rice, and fillings.
  • Uramaki-zushi: "Inside-out" rolls with rice on the outside and nori on the inside.
  • Chirashi-zushi: A scattered sushi bowl with rice and various toppings.
